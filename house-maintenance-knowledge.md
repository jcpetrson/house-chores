# Home Maintenance Master Checklist

House: ~6,000+ sq ft, built ~2000, Leesburg VA
Climate: Mid-Atlantic (hot/humid summers, cold winters with occasional ice/snow)
Dog: Yes (Bailey)
Owner capabilities: Skilled DIY, has wood shop

---

## Monthly

- [ ] Replace HVAC filters (more frequent with dog hair)
- [ ] Clean garbage disposal (ice + citrus or baking soda + vinegar)
- [ ] Run water in rarely-used fixtures to keep P-traps from drying out
- [ ] Test smoke and CO detectors (replace batteries 2x/year)
- [ ] Check water softener salt level (if applicable)
- [ ] Inspect under sinks for leaks or moisture
- [ ] Clean range hood filter
- [ ] Check sump pump operation (if applicable)

---

## Quarterly / Seasonal

### Q1 - Winter (January - March)

- [ ] Check for ice dams after heavy snow
- [ ] Inspect attic for condensation or frost
- [ ] Check exposed pipes in unheated spaces (garage, crawlspace) for insulation integrity
- [ ] Test water heater pressure relief valve
- [ ] Check for drafts around windows and doors
- [ ] Inspect weatherstripping and caulking on exterior doors
- [ ] Deep clean kitchen exhaust and vent
- [ ] Service snowblower / winter equipment

### Q2 - Spring (April - June)

- [ ] Walk full exterior: inspect siding, trim, foundation for winter damage
- [ ] Clean gutters and downspouts
- [ ] Inspect roof from ground (binoculars) for missing/damaged shingles
- [ ] Check grading around foundation - water should flow away from house
- [ ] Service AC / heat pump before cooling season (filter, coils, refrigerant)
- [ ] Pressure wash siding, driveway, walkways, deck
- [ ] Inspect deck/patio for rot, loose boards, popped fasteners
- [ ] Check exterior faucets and hose bibs for freeze damage
- [ ] Inspect and clean dryer vent (fire hazard)
- [ ] Open crawlspace vents (if applicable)
- [ ] Treat for termites / schedule pest inspection
- [ ] Test irrigation system, check heads and zones
- [ ] Touch up exterior paint and caulking
- [ ] Inspect and clean window wells
- [ ] Fertilize and treat lawn (aerate if needed)
- [ ] Mulch beds, trim shrubs away from house

### Q3 - Summer (July - September)

- [ ] Check attic ventilation and insulation (easiest to notice problems when it's hot)
- [ ] Inspect and clean bathroom exhaust fans
- [ ] Trim trees and shrubs away from house and roof
- [ ] Check and clean condensate drain line on AC
- [ ] Inspect garage door hardware, lubricate tracks and rollers
- [ ] Check caulking around windows and doors (exterior)
- [ ] Inspect and repair any cracked mortar on chimney or brick
- [ ] Power wash and reseal deck if needed
- [ ] Check driveway for cracks (seal if asphalt)

### Q4 - Fall (October - December)

- [ ] Clean gutters again after leaves drop
- [ ] Service furnace / heat pump before heating season
- [ ] Drain and disconnect outdoor hoses
- [ ] Shut off interior valves to exterior hose bibs
- [ ] Reverse ceiling fan direction (clockwise for winter)
- [ ] Inspect and replace weatherstripping as needed
- [ ] Close crawlspace vents (if applicable)
- [ ] Blow out irrigation system
- [ ] Test generator if applicable
- [ ] Check fireplace / chimney - schedule sweep if needed
- [ ] Inspect and clean window tracks
- [ ] Stock winter supplies (ice melt, sand)
- [ ] Check battery backup on sump pump and garage door opener

---

## Annual

- [ ] Have HVAC system professionally serviced (spring for AC, fall for heat)
- [ ] Professional chimney inspection and sweep
- [ ] Scope main sewer line with camera (especially with mature trees)
- [ ] Flush water heater to remove sediment
- [ ] Test and inspect all GFCIs and AFCIs
- [ ] Inspect electrical panel for signs of corrosion or overheating
- [ ] Check and update home inventory / insurance documentation
- [ ] Inspect attic for pest intrusion, insulation displacement, moisture
- [ ] Inspect crawlspace or basement for moisture, cracks, pest activity
- [ ] Deep clean dryer vent from exterior termination point
- [ ] Lubricate all door hinges, locks, and garage door springs
- [ ] Inspect all toilets for running, leaks at base, and flapper condition
- [ ] Test garage door auto-reverse safety feature
- [ ] Reseal natural stone countertops (if applicable)
- [ ] Service whole-house humidifier (if applicable)

## Capital / Lifecycle Items (Track Condition and Replacement Timeline)

These are not recurring tasks but items to evaluate and plan for based on age and condition.

| System | Typical Lifespan | Status at 26 Years | Notes |
|--------|-----------------|---------------------|-------|
| Roof (asphalt shingle) | 20-30 years | At or near end of life if original | Get professional inspection |
| HVAC system | 15-25 years | Likely replaced once already or due | Track efficiency decline |
| Water heater (tank) | 10-15 years | On 2nd or 3rd unit | Check anode rod annually on current unit |
| Windows | 20-30 years | Original windows likely losing efficiency | Check for fog between panes, draft |
| Exterior paint | 7-15 years | Multiple repaints by now | Inspect annually |
| Deck (wood) | 15-25 years | May need rebuild vs. repair assessment | Check structural posts and ledger board |
| Driveway (asphalt) | 20-30 years | May need resurfacing | Seal every 3-5 years |
| Garage door opener | 10-15 years | Likely on 2nd unit | Check safety features |
| Sump pump | 7-10 years | Replace proactively | Keep backup |
| Garbage disposal | 8-15 years | May be on 2nd unit | |
| Dishwasher | 10-15 years | Likely replaced | |
| Washer/dryer | 10-15 years | Likely replaced | |
| Cast iron drain pipes | 50-75 years | Fine for now | Scope if slow drains appear |
| Electrical panel | 25-40 years | Evaluate capacity and condition | |
